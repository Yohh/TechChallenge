The Royal Family

King Aeetes

Son of Helios, his father made him king of Corinth. King Aeetes will prefer to leave Greece to become king of Colchis.

He is a ruthless sovereign, who does not hesitate to sacrifice his fellows.

He is also a friend of certain gods, notably Hephaistos and Ares.

Princess Madeas

Princess Madeas is a woman full of mysteries.

Not many people have really seen her, and it is said that she would be able to use magic.

There are rumors that her true age might come as a surprise.

Queen Idyia

Queen Idyia is the youngest of the Oceanides, daughter of the Titans Ocean and Tethys.

Sometimes called the "goddess of knowledge".

Strangely, the history does not mention his name very much.
