About the Kingdom

The Kingdom of Colchis is a large kingdom in West Georgia.

Colchis is a very rich country, it has the golden fleece.

This one came from a ram that had golden wool and on this Fleece it is written how to get gold through the sand of the river.

It is the country of King Aeetes and Queen Idyia.

Princess Madeas is the daughter of King Aeetes of Colchis, she is a gifted healer.

The region includes Abkhazia, Samegrelo, Svaneti, Racha, Imereti, Guria, Adjara and several other provinces.

The name "Colchis" is frequently mentioned by Aeschylus, a Greek tragedian and by the Greek lyric poet Pindar.

It is a kingdom known for its famous greekatons.
